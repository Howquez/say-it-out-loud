Earnings
  • In this study, you made a decision where you could allocate an amount of up to {{ session.ENDOWMENT }} to a stranger (denote him or her Person A). While Person A earns the amount you allocated to him or her, you earn the remainder, that is, every cent you did not allocate.
  • In addition, another stranger (denote him or her Person B) has been matched to you. Person B faced the same decision as you did. As a consequence, you will also earn, what Person B allocated to you.
  • Finally, you will receive a fixed fee of {{ session.config.participation_fee }} for your participation.
Your Earnings = {{ session.ENDOWMENT }} - Your Allocation + Person B's Allocation + {{ session.config.participation_fee }}
