Decision & Earnings

In this part of the study we ask you to make one decision.

To this end, you are randomly matched with another participant, who also participates in the study. You can decide how much money to allocate to this person. Doing so, you can choose any amount between $0.00 and {{ session.ENDOWMENT }}.

Of that {{ session.ENDOWMENT }}, every cent that is not allocated to the other person will be transferred to your bank account at the end of the study. For example, if you allocate 0 cents to the other person, you will receive an additional bonus of {{ session.ENDOWMENT }} and the other person receives a bonus of $0.00. If you allocate {{ session.ENDOWMENT }} to the other person, you will receive a bonus of $0.00 and the other person will receive a bonus of {{ session.ENDOWMENT }}

Please note that your choice has an actual impact: Both you and the other person will be paid according to your choice.

Input

{{ if participant.interface == "Voice" }}

You will use your microphone to record your decision. To this end, you will see buttons that enable you to start, stop and submit your recording. In addition, you will see a fill-in-the-gap-text. Use it to communicate your decision. You can withdraw your recording and start a new one before you submit. {{ if participant.allowReplay }} You can listen to your recording. {{ else }} You cannot listen to your recording, though. {{ endif }}

{{ endif }} {{ if participant.interface == "Text" }}

You will use your keyboard to record your decision. To this end, you will see a text field. In addition, you will see a fill-in-the-gap-text. Use it to communicate your decision.

{{ endif }} {{ if participant.interface == "Dropdown" }}

You will use your mouse to record your decision. To this end, you will see a text dropdown menu.

{{ endif }} {{ if participant.interface == "Slider" }}

You will use your mouse to record your decision. To this end, you will see a slider.

{{ endif }}

Please Note:

  • You can choose any integer between 0 and 200 (cent).
  • Once you have submitted your decision, you cannot make any changes.

Procedure

After you have made your decision, you have to answer a short questionnaire. Once you have done so, you have finished this study, and we will start administering the payments.
