Instructions

You are endowed with {{ C.ENDOWMENT }}.

You can decide how many of your {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} to transfer to a charity. Doing so, you can choose any amount between 0 and {{ C.ENDOWMENT }}. The charity, the American Red Cross, receives the amount that you decide to transfer while you receive the amount that you decide not to transfer (and thus to keep). Please note that your decision has an actual impact: Both you and the American Red Cross will be paid according to your choice.

Procedure

Before you make your decision, we ask you to confirm that you have read and understand the instructions using your microphone. After you have made your decision, you have to answer a short questionnaire. Once you have done so, you have finished this study.

Decision

{{ if player.voice_interface }}

You will use your microphone to record your decision. To this end, you will see buttons that enable you to start, stop and submit your recording. In addition, you will see a fill-in-the-gap-text. Use it to communicate your decision. Note that you can withdraw your recording and start a new one before you submit. You cannot listen to your recording, though. Please make sure that one can understand your decision loud and clearly, while you confirm that you have read and understand the instructions.

{{ else }}

You will use your keyboard to record your decision. To this end, you will see a text input as well as a submit-button. You can enter any integer between 0 and 10 before you submit.

{{ endif }}
